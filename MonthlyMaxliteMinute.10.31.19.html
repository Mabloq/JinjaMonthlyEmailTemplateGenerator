LED HIGH BAY DIRECTFIT LAMPS
LED High Bay DirectFit Lamps are the plug-and-play, energy-saving replacements for HID bulbs in existing high bay fixtures. The lamp screws directly into Mogul-based sockets and operates off the existing ballast - no rewiring or extra labor required! Two models are available to cover most retrofit needs. The enclosed-rated (145W E145MH750) scales from 175W to 400W HID by using current sensing technology that adjusts the lumen output of the lamp to match the existing ballast. In other words, one lamp suits many installations!

The higher wattage 250W version (250MH750) replaces 750W - 1000W MH, and can only be used in non-enclosed fixtures. High Bay DirectFit lamps are the perfect solution for warehouses and other commercially leased spaces in which the benefits of LED are desired without the costs of installing permanent fixtures.

Features
• 145W (Enclosed rated) replaces 175W - 400W
• 250W (Non-enclosed) replaces 750W-1000W
• E39, EX39, E40 lamp base
• 5-year warranty
• 60,000 hours L70 rated lamp life
• 5000K

FIELDMAX HIGH OUTPUT FLOOD LIGHT
FieldMax™ High Output Flood Lights deliver maintenance-free, high quality lighting for sports and recreational fields (Class of Play III or IV), and other large outdoor spaces, such as car dealerships.
