LINEAR CID1 LUMINAIRE WITH EMERGENCY BATTERY BACKUP
The HL-LI SERIES linear LED luminaire with emergency battery backup is engineered to stand up to the toughest operating conditions in Class I Division 1 environments such as petroleum refineries and grain processing facilities. A slim profile and variety of mounting options enable this long-lasting, high-performance fixture to address virtually any lighting need in harsh and hazardous locations. As an added safety feature, the luminaire is equipped with integral emergency battery backup that keeps light on up to 90 minutes after the loss of power in environments up to -20 degrees Celsius.
