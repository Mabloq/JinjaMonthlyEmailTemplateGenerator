TRIMAX EARNS AWARD FROM EC&M MAGAZINE
The new TriMax Vapor Tight has been named a 2019 Product of the Year Category Winner by EC&M Magazine. One of only six luminaires recognized in the competition, the TriMax features a patented linking design that maintains a hermetic seal when two or more 4’ fixtures are linked together. By simply snapping a toolless connection bracket into place, installers can uniquely create an IP66 rated lighting solution that is 8’ or longer!

Category winners will go on to compete for the 2019 Product of the Year Platinum, Gold, and Silver awards, which will be determined through an upcoming online readers’ poll and announced in EC&M’s August 2019 issue. EC&M (Electrical Construction & Maintenance) is the technical authority for electrical professionals and engineers in the industrial and commercial space.
