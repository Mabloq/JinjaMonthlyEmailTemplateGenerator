EARN 100 PERCENT OVERAGE ON CLOSEOUT PRODUCTS!
MaxLite is introducing a new incentive plan for sales of Clearance products (LC10) designed to help you win more opportunities and earn more cash in the process!

Reps will now earn 3 percent commission on the floor price PLUS 100% overage on Clearance products. For example, if floor price is $10, and the product is sold for $15, total commission will be $5.30 (3% of $10 = $0.30, and 100% overage is $5.00). The Clearance and Sale Products tool has been updated to distinguish between products that are Clearance (LC10) and those that are on Sale (short-term special). This enables agents to view and sort available inventory of Clearance Products in real-time. Standard freight policy applies to Clearance products. All LC10 sales are final; no returns/exchanges except for manufacturing defects.
