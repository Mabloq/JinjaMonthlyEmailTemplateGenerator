REMINDER TO USE PROMO CODES ON STOCKING ORDERS
For LED Partnership Program stocking orders without a buy back, please continue to use the standard promo codes for each order amount from $5000 up (MAXSILVER, MAXGOLD, MAXPLATINUM).

For distributors who sign on with a buy back, please add “BB” to the end of the promo code on the order as follows: MAXSILVERBB, MAXGOLDBB, MAXPLATINUMBB. The buy back amount should also be included on the order; use part number #BUYBACK for the amount to be credited.

A recommended stocking list is available as a Flyer and Excel worksheet. All materials are archived under the Stocking Program banner at the top of Rep Resources.
