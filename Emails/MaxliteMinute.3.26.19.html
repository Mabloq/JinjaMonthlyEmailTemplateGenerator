ARCMAX OFFERED IN LOWER WATTAGES WITH DLC PREMIUM PERFORMANCE
MaxLite has added lower wattage, DLC Premium listed models to the ArcMax family of volumetric troffers. The line was extended with 20W, 2’x2’ (2,540 lumens) and 30W, 2’x4’ (3,810 lumens) products to meet the market need for energy savings and application preference for lower lumen LED fixtures. Delivering exceptional efficacy of 127 lumens per watt, the ArcMax produces voluminous light from an aesthetically appealing, single basket design. These models are currently in stock, with more inventory arriving over the next few weeks. A 1'X4' version is forthcoming. View the datasheet for complete specifications.

BLHE CROSSES OVER TO GEN 2
The BLHE Series Linear High Bay is moving to the next product generation; here is a quick guide to what’s changing. MaxLite is activating the new models when the corresponding wattages run out in Gen 1. DLC listing for the new generation is currently pending; data sheets will be updated as soon as certifications are completed (ETA April 1). Please contact your MaxLite customer relations manager with any questions.

Additional notes:
  • 162W from Gen 1 will become 178W in Gen 2. The 162W for Gen 2 can be produced as made to order only.
  • 223W from Gen 1 was a 4’ product; Gen 2 version is a 2’ product (lower price). The Gen 2 version will be in stock in late May. If you have projects that require 223W, please check if the customer can take the 265W at the same price as the 223W Gen 1. MaxLite will make this available until the Gen 2, 223W version is in stock.
