MAXLITE LAUNCHES LED SPOT LIGHT AS ADVANCED LIGHTING FOR HORTICULTURE APPLICATIONS
MaxLite announces the PhotonMax Spot Light as the first in a range of high-performance LED grow lights designed to optimize crop production in greenhouses and other controlled horticulture applications. Through innovative product design and engineering, the PhotonMax LED Spot Light (PH-GH Series) can match applications where a traditional 1000W HID fixture is used, but in a smaller form factor that is both aesthetically pleasing and extremely durable. The Spot Light is available in PPFs of 714-790 µmol/s (360W) or 1,233-1,358 µmol/s (600W), and four spectra tailored to achieve higher yields and higher quality in a variety of crop types. PHOTONMAX LED SPOT LIGHT FIRST PRODUCT APPROVED FOR DLC HORTICULTURAL QUALIFIED PRODUCTS LIST
MaxLite proudly announces the new PhotonMax LED Spot Light as the first product to be approved to the DesignLights Consortium (DLC) Horticultural Qualified Products List. Eight SKUs from the product series (PH-GH360, PH-GH600) are now listed to the Horticultural Qualified Products List, which may be viewed on the DLC website. Read the announcement. TRIMAX EARNS AWARD FROM EC&M MAGAZINE
The new TriMax Vapor Tight has been named a 2019 Product of the Year Category Winner by EC&M Magazine. One of only six luminaires recognized in the competition, the TriMax features a patented linking design that maintains a hermetic seal when two or more 4’ fixtures are linked together. By simply snapping a toolless connection bracket into place, installers can uniquely create an IP66 rated lighting solution that is 8’ or longer!

Category winners will go on to compete for the 2019 Product of the Year Platinum, Gold, and Silver awards, which will be determined through an upcoming online readers’ poll and announced in EC&M’s August 2019 issue. EC&M (Electrical Construction & Maintenance) is the technical authority for electrical professionals and engineers in the industrial and commercial space.
ARCMAX OFFERED IN LOWER WATTAGES WITH DLC PREMIUM PERFORMANCE
MaxLite has added lower wattage, DLC Premium listed models to the ArcMax family of volumetric troffers. The line was extended with 20W, 2’x2’ (2,540 lumens) and 30W, 2’x4’ (3,810 lumens) products to meet the market need for energy savings and application preference for lower lumen LED fixtures. Delivering exceptional efficacy of 127 lumens per watt, the ArcMax produces voluminous light from an aesthetically appealing, single basket design. These models are currently in stock, with more inventory arriving over the next few weeks. A 1'X4' version is forthcoming. View the datasheet for complete specifications.
BLHE CROSSES OVER TO GEN 2
